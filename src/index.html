Welcome to Battleship!

Strategically locate and sink your opponent's fleet before they destroy yours. Both players command a fleet placed on a 10×10 grid. Your objective is to find the location of the enemy ships and sink them before your fleet is destroyed.

Fleet Composition
  • Carrier — 5 cells
  • Battleship — 4 cells
  • Cruiser — 3 cells
  • Submarine — 3 cells
  • Destroyer — 2 cells
Rules of the Game
  • The game is played on a 10×10 grid.
  • Ships are placed randomly at the start of the game.
  • Ships cannot overlap or extend outside the board.
  • Click on a cell in the enemy board to attack.
  • A red cell indicates a hit and a grey cell indicates a miss.
  • Players take turns attacking one cell at a time.
  • The first player to sink all enemy ships wins the battle.

Good luck, Captain!
